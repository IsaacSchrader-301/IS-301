# Campfires & Fireplaces: A Warm Repository

Welcome to **Campfires & Fireplaces**, a repository dedicated to the art, science, and soul of fire — whether crackling under the stars or glowing in a hearth.

##  Why Fires Matter

Few things evoke comfort and connection like a well-tended flame. Whether you're:

- **Roasting marshmallows** under a starlit sky
- **Warming your toes** beside a stone fireplace
- Or simply **watching embers dance**, lost in thought

Fire brings people together.

##  What's Inside

This repository contains:

1. `firepit_designs.md` — Minimalist and rustic firepit blueprints
2. `fireplace_styles.md` — From colonial to contemporary hearths
3. `safety_tips.md` — How to enjoy fire responsibly
4. `stories_around_fire.md` — Anecdotes, myths, and memories
